Burlingame paving FAQ

What should I ask before hiring a Burlingame paving contractor?

Ask for base repair quantities, asphalt thickness, milling depth, drainage assumptions, ADA responsibility, striping details, warranty exclusions, and unit pricing for variable work.

How do I know whether my Burlingame parking lot needs repair or replacement?

If distress is isolated, repair may be enough. If alligator cracking, drainage failure, potholes, or base movement are widespread, use the Repair vs. Replace tool before approving another surface patch.

Why does ADA matter during paving in Burlingame?

Overlay, concrete, ramp, and striping work can change accessible routes, stall slopes, transitions, and access aisles. ADA should be reviewed before the project begins, not after striping is complete.
