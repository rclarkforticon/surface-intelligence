// Budget variables

What changes the paving number.

Condition

How far gone is the pavement?

Cracking, base failure, drainage problems, and age determine whether preservation, overlay, or reconstruction makes sense.

Scope

What else is tied to the work?

Striping, ADA stalls, concrete flatwork, ramps, phasing, permits, and access management can change the budget.

Timing

What happens if you wait?

Deferred maintenance can turn manageable repairs into larger replacement work with more disruption and cost.
