// Free Tools

Tools for real property decisions.

Use these before a board meeting, contractor call, budget request, or ADA concern. Each tool is designed to help property managers ask better questions, explain scope clearly, and plan reserves before approving expensive work.

60 seconds

Material Picker

Find out whether asphalt, concrete, a hybrid design, overlay, or replacement is the right starting point.

 2 minutes

Three-Bid Decoder

Compare three paving bids by useful life, scope quality, and cost per year — not just total price.

 Risk check

ADA Lawsuit Risk Scorecard

Spot common accessible parking and path-of-travel red flags before they turn into expensive exposure.

 Board packet

Board Meeting Language Generator

Turn contractor scope, ADA concerns, reserve pressure, or bid confusion into clear board-ready language.

 Reserve planning

HOA Pavement Reserve Estimator

Build a rough reserve range and monthly per-door planning number for future pavement work.
Not sure where to start? Use the Three-Bid Decoder when you have proposals in hand, the Board Meeting Language Generator before a vote, or send a project question if the scope is unclear.
